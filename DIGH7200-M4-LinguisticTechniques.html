Description Technique
This technique involves breaking down text into individual tokens, such as words or phrases, to analyze the language structure and meaning Tokenization
Stop words are commonly used words that can be removed from the text to focus on the more important words for analysis Stop Word Removal
This technique involves identifying the parts of speech, such as nouns, verbs, and adjectives, in a sentence or phrase to understand the context and meaning of the text Part-of-Speech (POS) Tagging
NER is used to identify and classify named entities in text, such as people, organizations, and locations, to extract meaningful information Named Entity Recognition
This technique is used to analyze the grammatical structure of a sentence and identify the relationships between words Dependency Parsing
This involves reducing words to their base or dictionary form, which can help with analysis by grouping similar words together Lemmatization
This technique involves reducing words to their root form, which can help with analysis by grouping related words together Stemming
This technique involves identifying and extracting the emotions and opinions expressed in text, which can be useful for understanding customer feedback and public opinion Sentiment Analysis
